# The Asado Forecast

The live shared page everyone refreshes:
- Big verdict for the host's location, updating every 15 minutes as models refine
- Countdown to the start time
- A stacked list of attendees with their individual verdicts as little colored dots (green/amber/red). You can see at a glance: "host is green, but three guests across town are amber because a cell is heading their way."
- A single "trend" indicator: is the forecast getting better or worse since the invite went out? This is the thing people actually want to know the morning of.
- One-tap Re-propose for the host: if the verdict degrades, suggest a new time window (same day +3h, or tomorrow) and everyone gets a push to re-confirm.
