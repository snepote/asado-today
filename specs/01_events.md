# Define an event

It allows a user to create the BBQ event, specifying the date, time, and location. The system will then use this information to provide a recommendation on whether it's a good idea to have a BBQ at that time and place, based on the weather forecast and other relevant factors.

Host flow: Once you've dialed in a verdict you like (day + hour + location), tap Convocar. You get a share link + a share card for WhatsApp. No account needed to create, no account needed to join. The asado now has a permanent short URL and lives until 24h after the event.
