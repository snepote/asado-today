# Recommendation screen

Based on date and location, the app will provide a recommendation on whether it's a good day to make a BBQ. The recommendation will be based on various factors such as weather conditions, wind direction, and sunset time. The screen will display a clear message indicating whether it's a good day for BBQ or not, along with relevant details that influenced the recommendation.

## Screen
- One main element displaying the sky icon, eg: sunny, cloudy, rainy, etc.
- A text with the recommendation: "Yes" / "No" / "Maybe"
- 4 small boxes bellow, showing main aspects of the recommendation, eg: "Temp: 28ºC", "Precipitation: 20%", "Wind: 15 km/h", "Sunset: 7:30 PM"
