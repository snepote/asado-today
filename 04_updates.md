# Asado event updates

The share card regenerates with live stats — if someone opens the WhatsApp preview 6 hours later, the numbers are current, not stale
- Vibe check — a tiny emoji-only thread under the forecast. No text chat (not the app's job). Just "🔥", "🥩", "🍷", "☔️😩". Keeps the energy without becoming Slack.
- Umbrella alert — if rain probability crosses 40% within 2h of start, every confirmed guest gets a push: "Asado at Martín's — bring a jacket, 50% rain at 19:00."
- Auto-verdict lock — 2 hours before start, the verdict "locks" so people stop anxiously refreshing. Host can override.
- Post-event: one tap to save the asado to history. Builds a personal log — "you've hosted 14 asados this year, 11 verdicts were SÍ, 2 were rescheduled, 1 happened anyway in the rain."
